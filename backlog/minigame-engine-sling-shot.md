# Mini-game engine — sling-shot

## Problem / opportunity

Aim-and-fling feeding feels playful and motor-rich without timers or fail states.

## Proposed feature

`MinigameEngine` `slingShot` + `SlingShotView`: pull back, launch snack at the animal.
Misses reload — always awards play XP.
