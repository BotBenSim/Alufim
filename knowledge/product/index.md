# Product - UX and Product Decisions

Why the experience is shaped the way it is: layout, flow, and interaction choices that
affect how a child (or parent) uses the app. Distinct from *engineering* decisions
([`../technical/`](../technical/index.md)) and *learning theory*
([`../educational/`](../educational/index.md)) — though a product choice often cites both.

## File shape

Shared OKF frontmatter (`id`, `title`, `status`, `date`, `tags`) plus `related`, with
sections: **Problem -> Decision -> User impact -> Trade-offs -> Signals to watch**. See
[`TEMPLATE.md`](TEMPLATE.md).
