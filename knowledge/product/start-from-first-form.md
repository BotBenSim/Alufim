# Character picker and play show a living baby, not an egg

## Problem

Starting as an egg delayed the “my animal” feeling and made early play feel like waiting.

## Decision

Present animals from their first living art (asset `*-2.png`) everywhere: home picker, run
header, collection unlock preview, and evolution ladder.

## User impact

Kids immediately see a creature they can bond with. Evolution still changes appearance across
three living stages.

## Trade-offs

Egg art remains on disk but unused. Players with old mental model of “hatch the egg” lose that
metaphor.

## Signals to watch

Confusion about “where did the egg go?”; whether three stages still feel like enough growth.
